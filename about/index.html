About

Hi! My name is Amy, and I'm a big fan of technical and creative exeperimentation, I do this by participating in Gamejams and small projects in my spare time.
One day I hope to have developed a game for every single game genre, using as many different skills as possible.
I love learning new things, from game engines and programming all the way to fun projects using an Arduino.
I aspire to expand my knowledge of programming through hard work and self-directed study and my ultimate goal is to have a breadth of knowledge in every field of the games industry and excel in programming.
I am motivated to bring accessibility to video games to support people with different disabilities as I believe there is currently a lack of accessible video games and accessories.
